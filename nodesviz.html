Legend

You can use this site to search and find out about music museums and archives that are of interest to you using keywords or specific phrases.This maybe by:

  • Place – country or city
  • Genre of music – jazz or hip-hop
  • Sites you can go and visit - British Music Experience or South Australian Jazz Archive
  • Platform – online sites or social media pages

You can also ‘drill down’ into the data. So if you wanted to find say, all the jazz museums in the United States and then all the jazz museums you can physically visit in the United States, and then all the jazz museums you can visit in New Orleans you will be able search and retrieve this information.
